Saving Account Overview

A savings account is a type of deposit account that allows you to save money while earning interest on your deposits. It provides a safe and secure way to store your funds and build your savings over time.

Features of Saving Account

  • No monthly maintenance fees
  • Competitive interest rates
  • Online banking access
  • ATM card for easy access to funds

Benefits of Saving Account

  • Build emergency fund
  • Save for short-term goals
  • Earn interest on your savings
  • Easy access to funds
